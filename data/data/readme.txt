Meta = Zymo Metagenomic data 
Isolate = bacterial isolate data
Truth = truth genomes from Refseq references or hybrid Nanopore/Illumina assembly
Draft = Medaka polished genomes
Homopolish = Homopolished genomes
e.g., Meta_Isolates_R9.4_homopolish.tar.gz includes the Homopolished genomes of metagenomic and isolate R9.4 datasets
